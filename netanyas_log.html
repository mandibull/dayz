Personal Log Last Entry: Cpt. Netanya Keitner

When I think about it now, it.. it all makes sense. If you really feel like we are not using our full potential, I mean, just go for it. If you really have the power to change the world in a way you believe is right, hell, just go for it. At this point, I dont really care. My name is Netanya Keitner and you probably want to know what the fuck I am talking about. To me it feels really surreal to actualy live up to this day, but here I am, sitting in a dark freezing corridor of an abandonned submarine, blizzard is raging outside, I dont think I can go back to the island now. And frankly, I dont want to. This was my last chance to get to talk to someone, but does not seem like anyone is here nor anything in here is usable.

Well, where to begin? It started in June of this year. We were poking the DPRK to see what they are up to on the China borders. Those CSAT guys are getting too scary, but whatever. It was nothing compared to what started happening on some useless island in Bering Sea. Joined USMC and Russian Federation forces were unable to stop a (supposedly) privately funded organization from experiments with extraterrestrial structure, found on Namalsk few years ago. Now this has really blew our intel through the roof, absolutely zero ideas how these guys have managed to find the gap, but they did and it was really getting too hot in there. Apparently, they have made a discovery. A discovery that - I can say safely now - wont be ever surpassed by the mankind again, a gateway to a completely new frontiers, an opportunity to break free from our daily habits, but if only it was understood correctly by them. Not that it would probably matter as they seemed to leave it for themselves anyway. Some top black ops shit was happening years prior the first boom. I cannot believe how this was overlooked, almost seems like it was deliberate from the top places. But its too late to judge anyone really at this point. They were sure prepared to sacrifice many things.

But the whole situation proves that they were (are??) just people like everyone else on this piece of rock in the middle of nowhere (talking about Earth here). Sometime you can pull whatever you have at your disposal, but in the end, its all about the individual people pressing buttons. One of those button pressing were in this submarine on June 12th, 2011, 0737 local time, Russian captain of SKAT-12 launched a nuclear warhead, targeted to strike still functioning Athena-1 facility. Everyone was determined that this plan B is the ONLY solution to get rid of NAC playing with their toys. Athena-1, already heavily damaged by the ongoing conflict, but still functioning, was operated by the remaining NAC staff, determined to reach their goal to utilize the extraterrestrial structure for opening up an artificially created wormhole. These Namalsk corp guys (an obvious cover-up name btw), were tough, almost seems like some kind of brainwash, but none really had any personal encounters with them, so hard to say. Anyway, during the (supposed) final moments of the conflict, they have actually managed to make a stable connection. From the logs I have found it seemed that they have actually opened it for few seconds, managed to send a drone inside. Someone logged "an alien looking landscape with a huge gas giant on the horizon, everyone stand frozen looking at the displays" so that kinda means mission success (I guess). But shortly after that, the warhead was released above Athena-1, with the wormhole connection sucking up most of the released energy, turning the other pretty alien landscape into a wasteland. The energy transfer destabilized the connection and shortly after, wormhole collapsed. Athena-1 power source finally (after all those days of beating it got from NAC, but also from US/Russia) overloaded, resulting in a massive underground explosion and collapse. The experiment was no more. You may ask, was that anyone's "home" there? Why it was opened at that particular place. Surely, this is no coincidence, the structure had to be a connector and we just sent a gift to someone. What we will get back in return? One can only speculate, but you know what? I feel like we already got one.

Everyone were super confused after all this has happened. Obviously, none had a clue what those NAC guys were actually doing there (we learned that later and waaay too late), but things end up in a good way it seemed. Namalsk was secured by newly formed TF Bering and we started digging into CSAT again. Everything was going well until 21st July, when some unknown forces stormed Russian defences on Namalsk, supposedly took something from the Athena RI, killing 41 soldiers in the process. Now that we had much more eyes on Namalsk, we figured out that the forces in question indeed had ties with CSAT. Now to this point, we dont really know if CSAT is behind NAC and all the shit they did, but we assume that they did not. But they may have gotten some intel from someone. Anyway, back in DPRK, things got a lot more busy. We had to deploy into DPRK directly as the Musan Launch Complex in Kangyee and Chachang started to become crazy active. Unfortunately, things did not go well at all, they have launched something into space and we have lost one of our operators, blowing the whole operation in DPRK. Rest of us barely got from there alive and during our departure, we learned that TF Bering HQ on Shemya was under the CSAT siege.

But things were about to get a lot busier.. our "little" wars becoming completely irrelevant. While our guys were trying to fight off CSAT on Shemya and number of other places, I have got assigned to Namalsk, because they have managed to find a way into some previously closed sections of Athena-1 and Athena-2 objects (we suspect that there is also Athena-3, but we have not been able to locate it yet). I was there to secure any sort of intel related to the NAC. The obvious CSAT focus on Namalsk, DPRK in-orbit experiments, it was getting too much for us. And well, if you have read my log, you probably know that we learned quite a lot about Namalsk, but at this point in time, I dont think it really matters. To this day, we dont know, what CSAT is up to, well, assuming they still exist. But someone had to orchestrate this and I feel like they are still out there somewhere, smiling at the technological breakthroughs they have managed (ignoring the fact that the mankind has collapsed).

Back to what happened though, astronomy is not really a my thing, but this (along with an obvious raging conflict) has made its appereance in the news - Pan-STARRS telescopes located a crazy fast object on parabolic trajectory, quickly designated as interstellar and given name of "Arakkis". Scientists were trying to figure out how such a fast object could still hold up and have such a low reflectivity across the spectrum. Then another shock came in after they have estimated the trajectory. It was a roughly 1 million slingshot of Earth. What are the chances of an interstellar object flying that close to Earth? It was at this point we started realizing the connection between the things that happened on Namalsk and Arakkis almost crashing into us. Only handful of people really knew what this could be, rest was praying it is really going to miss. That would most-likely be a doomsday event. Well, expect that it was exactly what Arakkis delivered to us in the end, indirectly. By the look of it, it was just a highly sophisticated interstellar carrier missile. Observers were able to identify separation of low-reflective pods when close to the trajectory pericenter. We are almost certain that these pods were set of a planet-wide busters/paralyzers. Later that day, reports of a highly contagious airborne agent started coming from Guanshin district in China. After that, things went downhill very fast. Chaos, hundreds of millions of people dead.

At that time, I was still on Namalsk. Hearing the stories from all around the world was something completely out of this world. Then our contacts slowly stopped responding. And then that shit has got to Namalsk somehow. I did not know what to do, do not really want to talk about what happened next either. But lets sum it up by saying that I did not get infected. I do not know why, but at this point, I dont think I can be considered as one being lucky. Its 52 days after the nerve agent has appeared, its crazy cold in here and I just dont know what to do really. The fact that I am still alive means that perhaps there are others too. The nerve agent may not have worked on everyone, which means whoever did this, they are not god-like. Something positive. I guess.
