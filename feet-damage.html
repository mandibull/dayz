Shoes Damage

Shoes damage every 10 steps = surface modifier.

Example: 100 steps on rock = 10 * 0.702 = 7 shoes damage.

Feet Bleed Chance

One feet (randomly picking left or right) bleed probability every 10 steps = movement modifier * surface modifier.

Examples:

  • you can't bleed if you walk
  • every 10 sprint steps on rock you have a 70% chance of a foot bleeding
  • every 10 run steps on rock you have a 35% chance of a foot bleeding
  • every 10 sprint steps on grass you have a 2.3% chance of a foot bleeding
  • every 10 run steps on grass you have a 1.65% chance of a foot bleeding
  • probability of at least one foot bleeding after n-steps = 1 - (1 - speed modifier * surface modifier) ^ (n / 10)
