DayZ melee values

These are the raw melee types and not the items themselves.

As a reference canned food have the MeleeBlunt damage type.

Infected "stealth" kills

To perform infected one shot kill player must:

  • heavy attack with proper weapon OR melee using firearm with a bayonet
  • be in a 45° angle behind target
  • hit head, neck or torso

Weapons:

  • BaseballBat
  • BrassKnuckles
  • Cleaver
  • CombatKnife
  • FirefighterAxe
  • Hammer
  • HuntingKnife
  • KitchenKnife
  • Machete
  • NailedBaseballBat
  • Pickaxe
  • Pipe
  • PipeWrench
  • Screwdriver
  • Shovel
  • Sickle
  • SledgeHammer
