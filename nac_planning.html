Project Athena

Project Athena is a codename for a high risk but high gain project, which primarily aims to utilize newly discovered alien structure on island of Namalsk to create a first ever artificial wormhole.

There are many phases of this project, this document will go through all of them in detail. Describing what has been already done and what is yet to be done. Project Athena is managed by our sub-organization called Namalsk Corporation (NAC). This rogue organization groups all the neccessary divisions, which are conducting operations on Namalsk or supporting the Namalsk operations from other places around the world. Important part plays the security division, because we expect NAC running into minor troubles with Russian goverment sooner or later.

Please note that this document is still work-in-progress as we reach new milestones within the project Athena.



0 Initial covert research

Our researches have conducted an extensive research on several places within the Bering sea, which eventualy led to a triangulation of an island called Namalsk. Triangulation was done based on detecting very weak signs of laterral xen radiation, which seems to be emitted in regular weak burst from already mentioned alien structure on Namalsk. This confirms that the structure is probably still somehow functional.

The access to the actual site of the structure was not a complicated given the state in which Namalsk currently is (inhabitant relocation, none really cared what we did). We have been able to remove upper parts of the ground on the western ridge of southern mountains and finally located what we have been search for months now. Weird looking structural parts with very biological look. Radar scans revealed that the parts could be as deep as 200 meteres. And by drilling several test boreholes, we have only confirmed radar scans and located quite extensive underground system, which on itself looks just unreal. The whole structure including the underground has a very organic feel. By analyzing the overall extent of the structure, we think that a long time ago, there was a seed structure that impacted Earth's surface and later growth itself into what we see right now. We have found number of devices in there, some of then indicating by a sound and/or a visual output that they still work somehow.



1 Location problem

Target structure is located on island of Namalsk. This island is located within the borders of Russian Federation, currently targeted by a total inhabitant relocation programme, launched in early 90s to centralize distant abandonned regions of Russian Federation. During the times of Cold War, Namalsk was an important place to USSR because of its close position to USA borders. Number of key military installations were built by that time. During 60s, major source of uranium ore was found in the Sebjan valley area and an important uranium mine was built on top of this deposit. While Namalsk has seen a great activity during the Cold War, after the fall of USSR, most of the operations on Namalsk were halted and and approx. 1500 of inhabinats basically left living within still un-finished town of Vorkuta or within its surroundings on the northen part of the island.

We have conducted several steps to gain a significant influence on Namalsk, while retaining some degree of covert operations on the southern part. This has eventualy led to the complete closure of southern parts of Namalsk. Leaving only the road to Sebjan airfield and Tara island open (but closely guarded). Keeping this under control is surely not an easy task, but the fact that many inhabinats do not agree with the goverment's relocation programme, we have gained many supporters within the local community. This has eventually led to a programme, focused on recruiting local inhabinats into our lines.



2 Asset relocation and infrastructure setup

In order to conduct any serious experiments on the southern part of the island, we had to increase our staff, budget and transportation capabilities. Until 2003, research was conducted mostly within the old Cold War research complex above the abandonned Sebjan uranium mine. Since then, we have increased the number of shipments to Tara harbor and utilized the local railway to transport major amount of assets to the central part of southern Namalsk. Thanks to that, additional facilities were refurbished and changed to our needs along with a completely new ones built. To support additional power requirements by our research, we have successfully restarted an old coal power plant within the Sebjan mine complex.

As of now (2008), there are three main research complexes:

- Object Athema-1 (A1) is the core of our research. We have used an old Cold War underground warehouse, which we have extended that it actually connects to the actual alien structure - more precisely with its underground. Every important experiment with the alien structure itself is happening within the A1 underground laboratories. This underground is accessible from the Athena Research Institute and A1 service tunnel entrance.

- Athena Research Institute is a complex, sitting on top of Sebjan valley, where we conduct all support operations for A1 and A2 research. It also hosts number of minor research facilities (basicaly any research that can be done outside of our underground laboratories) and a huge warehouse.

- Object Athena-2 (A2) is focused on the A1 research applications, currently focused at developing advanced FLAX capacitors, which will enable us further advance experiments at A1/A3 facilities. It is based on old Cold War underground communication station, which was heavily modified to include a prototype FLAX reactor.

- Object Athena-3 (A3) is currently being constructed. We will attempt replicating the results of A1 research using our own tech there.
